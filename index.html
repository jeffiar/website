About me

I am an undergraduate studying physics at Stanford University. Broadly, I am intrigued by the physics of the biochemical world -- the thermodynamics of life, the quantum chemistry of vision, the dynamical landscape of macromolecules, and more. Currently, I'm doing research about the photophysics of fluorescent proteins in the Boxer lab.

I play horn in the Stanford Collaborative Orchestra, and I improvise music on the Stanford Improv team. I also play piano in a jazz combo called Casual Business.

On my website, you can find a list of my upcoming performances, as well as assorted thoughts, musical compositions, coding projects, and more.

You can contact me at jeffjar (snail) stanford.edu .
