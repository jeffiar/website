Nature is Clever

Yesterday during lunch, I was enjoying a pleasant conversation on the balcony of the dining hall. At the table next to us, some people went back inside to get more food, leaving their plates outside. All of a sudden, a crow came in out of nowhere, stole a cookie in its beak, and flew off! Mother Nature is quite clever.

-Jeffrey Chang, May 12 2019.
